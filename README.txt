No coding is required. Suitable for all kinds of websites that require a single video player to a large video-sharing website like YouTube/Vimeo.
Now you can convert any post to video right from your WordPress dashboard just in 3 easy steps.
 
### Step -1: Generate Your API key
In order to start rendering videos, you have to register on our [official website](https://wpvideobaker.com/). After completing registration, you will find your API key on your [profile page](https://wpvideobaker.com/my-account/). You will need it on the welcome page of the plugin to go ahead.
 
### Step -2: The Global Settings
Here you have to give a global style setup for all your videos. WP Video Baker Admin panel allows you to set up backgrounds, colors, and fonts for different video elements. Make your videos more branded from here.
After that, set up your branded opener and ending setting for your videos. And you are all set to render your videos.
 
### Step -3: Build Your Video and Rendering It 
This section appears at the bottom of all your post pages in the editor. This is where most of the work is done. Here you can break your post into video sections, add images, titles, and subtitles, and set up an opener and a thank-you section text and voiceover.
 
After finishing the text, images, and section settings, save it and refresh the page to preview your video. Finally, just click on **Generate Videos**. Your video will be set to your provided email address in minutes.
 
The easiest way to turn your WordPress blogs into beautiful videos. Now, share your videos and skyrocket your sales.

== Installation ==
This section describes how to install WP Video Baker and get it working.
e.g.
Install using one of these options:
**Method 1:** Install directly from the WordPress Admin panel: go to Plugins -> Add New -> Search for “WP Video Baker,” and click the Install button.
 
**Method 2:** Download the ZIP manually from WordPress’ plugins repository and upload it through the WordPress Admin panel: go to Plugins -> Add New -> Upload Plugin, browse to the downloaded Zip and upload it.
 
**Method 3:** Download the ZIP, extract it and manually upload the extracted folder through FTP to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation.
